Features

Everything your team needs

One platform to plan, track, and collaborate — without the chaos of juggling six different tools.

  • Unified task board

    Kanban, list, and timeline views in one place. See every task across every project without switching screens.

  • Real-time collaboration

    Comment, mention, and react to tasks in real time. Everyone stays in sync whether they're remote or in the office.

  • Smart automations

    Automate repetitive work with no-code rules. Trigger actions when tasks move, deadlines approach, or assignees change.

    Understand velocity, blockers, and team load at a glance. Make better decisions with data, not gut feelings.
